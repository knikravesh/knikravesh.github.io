In A Tournament of Lies three lifelong friends attempts to rank every single video game ever made on an ever expanding list. Is Ocarina of Time better than Half-Life? Is Duke Nukem Forever worse than Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 5? Listen to hear the list reveal its mysteries!

This app was made so that the podcast's dedicated viewers and listeners can easily enojy all the content on offer, in one place, while on the move.

You get involved by contributing your own submissions, either via the app's suggestion tool or via email.

lies@atournamentoflies.com
